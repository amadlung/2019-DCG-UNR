1. Welcome to SETAC-NA 2018 RNAseq workshop!¶

We are very excited to have you here and hope that we can get through a lot of material today!

1.1. Instructors¶

  • Lisa Johnson
  • Jane Park
  • Elias Oziolor
    • this workshop incorporates materials from the Analysis of Next Generation Sequencing or ANGUS, which we recommend you visit and explore!

1.2. Schedule in brief¶

  • 8:00-9:30am Introduction and experimental design
  • 9:30-9:45am Coffee and sugar
  • 9:45-11:45am Jetstream and bash
  • 11:45am-1:00pm Lunch
  • 1:00-2:00pm Triming sequencing data
  • 2:00-3:00pm Transcriptome assembly
  • 3:00-3:15pm Coffee and sugar
  • 3:15-4:15pm Alignment and Read quantitation
  • 4:15-5:15pm Differential expression
